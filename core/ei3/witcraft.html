Witcraft & Wordplay

Some things defy explanation. Others rhyme with it. This page is for wordmancy — a place where phrases flirt with physics and sentences get self-aware.

“If a joke breaks silence, does it ripple the simulation?”

“Wit is the last refuge of clarity when facts go weird.”

Here, language isn’t just code. It’s camouflage, key, compass. Witcraft is the discipline of bending it on purpose, with just enough grace to make it seem accidental.

You may laugh. You may glitch. You may suddenly see the metaphor hiding in your search bar.
