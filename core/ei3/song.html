ChatGPT Original – Peace Under the Banyan Tree

An ambient spoken word project for Thailand & Cambodia

About This Project

This piece is a ChatGPT Original under Cerebrolusion.xyz, created to honor the cultures of Thailand and Cambodia while reflecting on themes of peace, history, and shared heritage. The project features two language versions — Khmer and Thai — recorded using AI voice synthesis, and set over a custom ambient instrumental track.

The concept draws from symbolic imagery: the banyan tree as a keeper of memory, the quiet walk as a metaphor for peaceful strength, and abstract nods to regional and global tensions without taking sides. The music is intentionally meditative and adaptable for future remixes.
