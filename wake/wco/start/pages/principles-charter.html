Principles Charter

The WCO operates by a simple compact of principles:

  • Voluntary Participation – Anyone may join, observe, or create. No obligation.
  • Neutral Channel – No endorsement, censorship, or ranking. Presence speaks for itself.
  • Scalability – Issues may be local, national, or global — each is valid.
  • Resolution Optional – Submissions do not need to reach consensus or outcome. Continuity itself has value.
  • Government Inclusion – Municipalities, agencies, and officials may engage or ignore; either choice is recorded.
  • No Central Authority – No leader, hierarchy, or binding control. Leadership emerges only through visibility.
  • Respect for Sovereignty – WCO does not override governments; it witnesses alongside them.

This charter is not a constitution. It is a baseline compact, a guarantee that the WCO is open, neutral, and scalable. Every node, mirror, or fork may display these principles as its foundation.
